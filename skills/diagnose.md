Diagnose where the job search funnel is breaking and give a specific protocol to fix it.

Steps:
1. Read `7-Situations/WHEN_STUCK.md` for the diagnostic framework
2. Ask the user these three questions, one at a time:
   - Are you sending outreach consistently? How many per week roughly?
   - Are you getting interview conversions? What's your rough reply or interview rate?
   - Are conversations converting to next rounds or offers?
3. Based on their answers, identify the exact situation from WHEN_STUCK.md
4. Give them the specific protocol for that situation — not generic advice, the exact steps from the playbook
5. If they mention objections in interviews, read `7-Situations/OBJECTION_PLAYBOOK.md` and give them the framework for their specific objection type
